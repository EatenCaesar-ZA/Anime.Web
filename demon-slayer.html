DEMON SLAYER

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge. It follows Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered and his younger sister is turned into a demon. The series explores themes of friendship, sacrifice, and the consequences of one's actions.
